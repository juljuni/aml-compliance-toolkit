## Research Methodology

**Sources Used:**
- 40+ web searches across official government, professional, legal, and commercial sources
- Focus on Australian-specific legislation and guidance
- Emphasis on practical compliance requirements for small accounting firms
- Reviewed both free and commercial compliance solutions
- Analyzed real-world cost impacts and time requirements

**Research Limitations:**
- Some commercial software pricing not publicly disclosed (quote-based)
- Full legislation text (PDF) experienced timeout errors during fetch attempts
- AUSTRAC websites experienced timeout errors during some fetch attempts
- Relied on official government summaries and professional firm analyses where full text unavailable

**Reliability:**
- All key findings cross-referenced across multiple authoritative sources
- Official AUSTRAC guidance prioritized over third-party interpretations
- Professional body resources (CPA, CA ANZ, IPA) used to validate practical application
- Legal firm analyses (MinterEllison, Grant Thornton) used for expert interpretation

**Currency:**
- Research conducted: February 14, 2026
- Regulations effective: July 1, 2026
- All sources checked for currency and accuracy as of research date
- Recommend re-checking AUSTRAC website closer to July 1, 2026 for any late-breaking updates
